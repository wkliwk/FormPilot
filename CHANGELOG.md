## 2026-03-30 — v1.0 Launch

FormPilot is live. Upload any PDF, Word doc, or photo of a paper form — get every field explained in plain language and auto-filled from your profile.

- **PDF & Word upload** — upload forms up to 10 MB; text is extracted locally for privacy
- **Image upload** — photograph a paper form (PNG, JPEG, WEBP, HEIC) and let AI read it
- **AI field explanations** — every field gets a plain-language description and example answer
- **Autofill from profile** — store your personal details once; FormPilot fills matching fields with confidence scores
- **Guided fill mode** — step through fields one at a time with in-context help
- **PDF export** — download a filled, submission-ready PDF
- **Chrome extension** — detect and autofill web forms directly in your browser
- **Free tier** — 5 forms per month at no cost; Pro plan unlocks unlimited forms
